About Fenoglio-org

Fenoglio-org is run by Ferruccio Fenoglio, a consultant and project coordinator working across cultural innovation, partnership building and project-specific collaboration networks.

Working approach

  • Build networks around specific project goals, with clear deliverables and shared responsibilities.
  • Combine coordination, strategic framing and creative-sector orientation for cultural and digital projects.
  • Target practical outcomes for small museums, rural cultural operators and cross-sector innovation teams.

Experience highlight

Project reference: NEAR/ANK/2021/EA-RP/0005 (IPA II – Competitiveness and Innovation). Role: Team Leader Technical Assistance. Managed coordination in a €1.7M programme with international partners and regional institutions.

Collaboration model

Fenoglio-org works with flexible project-specific teams. We recruit cross-disciplinary contributors based on project size and scope, while maintaining a consistent coordination and quality oversight layer.

Value is created by enabling small teams to deliver coherent strategy, accessible interaction design, and museum-oriented digital experiences.
