Let's start a conversation

Fenoglio-org is open to conversations with museums, heritage organisations, cultural partners, local authorities, associations, schools, and community groups.

We are especially interested in collaborations around European projects, museum engagement, and community-led content creation for museums and other shared public spaces.

What you can contact us about

You may want to get in touch if you are looking for:

  • a partner for a Creative Europe or cooperation project
  • support in shaping a museum engagement idea
  • a pilot collaboration around MuseGame
  • facilitation of local groups to create stories, routes, interpretation, or other content for museums and community spaces

How to reach us

The easiest way to contact us is through the partnership form.
