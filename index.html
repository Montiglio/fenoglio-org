What Fenoglio-org does

We coordinate and connect cross-disciplinary teams around cultural and innovation calls, rural museum initiatives and creative-content interaction projects, delivering project framing and network-based delivery.

Focus areas

European partnership coordination

Structuring collaboration across countries, sectors and funding frameworks.

Cultural and creative industries

Supporting cultural operators with digital heritage and creative production pathways.

Small rural museums

Targeted approaches for small and medium institutions with limited technical resources.

Digital interaction experiences

Designing accessible tablet and hybrid interaction solutions for visitors.

Graphic and interaction design

Visual identity and interaction tooling that is simple, flexible and usable.
