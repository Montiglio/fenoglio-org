MuseoGame

MuseoGame is an open-source digital tool designed to help museums transform their cultural content into simple interactive games accessible to visitors on tablets inside museum spaces. The project addresses a common challenge faced by small and medium-sized museums across Europe: the difficulty of creating engaging digital experiences without technical expertise, large budgets, or complex technological infrastructures.

What problem it addresses

Small museums often lack the resources to develop visitor engagement tools. MuseoGame focuses on accessible interaction with curated content, reducing dependency on large technical teams or custom development.

Who it is for

Small and medium-sized museums, cultural heritage operators, and project partners seeking low-barrier digital interaction enhancements for onsite visitor experience.

Why small and rural museums matter

These institutions preserve local identity and need tailored solutions that allow respect for local narratives while offering engaging digital visitor touchpoints.

Why this approach is different

  • Simple � minimal steps to transform existing content into a playable experience.
  • Accessible � tablet-first design with clear usability guidelines and lightweight deployment.
  • Low infrastructure � works offline or on local intranet setups.
  • Reusable � content and interaction templates are adaptable across collections.
  • Open-source direction � enabling shared development and transparent community contribution.
